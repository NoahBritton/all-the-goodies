# 06 · Dimensions

**Pack:** ATM10 · MC 1.21.1 · NeoForge. Recipe/access facts marked **✅ JEI** / verify in-game.

ATM10's "dimension progression" is really an **ore-tier progression** layered onto adventure
dimensions: each ATM ore is gated behind a different dimension (see
[02 · Progression Ladder](02-progression-ladder.md)).

## Dimension table

| Dimension (mod) | How to get there | Why you go (key resource) | Dangers |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Deep Dark / Ancient City** (vanilla + Deeper and Darker) | Dig below ~Y -40 in the Overworld | **Allthemodium** ore + Allthemodium templates (brush Suspicious Clay) | **Warden** (one-shots early gear); darkness |
| **Mining Dimension** (AllTheMods) | Craft a **Teleport Pad** (Allthemodium Nuggets), place in Overworld, step on it | Bulk ores — multiple layers, 37+ ore types; densest resource source | Open caverns, mobs, falls |
| **Nether** (vanilla) | Nether Portal | **Vibranium** (near ceiling), **Piglich Heart** (alloys/Star), Bastion templates | Vibranium is high = lava falls; Piglich is tough |
| **The End** (vanilla) | End Portal | **Unobtainium** (End Highlands), Dragon Egg, Shulkers | Void; Ender Dragon; island traversal |
| **The Other** (Deeper and Darker) | Place a **second Teleport Pad in the Nether**, step on it | **Unobtainium smithing templates** (dungeon chests — *only* source); Vibranium; **Heart of the Deep** (Star) | Very dark, hostile sculk, easy to get lost |
| **Twilight Forest** (Twilight Forest) | Throw a **diamond into a 2×2 water pool ringed by flowers** | 8-boss mid-game loot chain; ironwood/steeleaf/knightmetal gear | Boss-gated; dark forest blocked until **Lich** is dead |
| **Eternal Starlight** (Eternal Starlight) | Build portal, activate with **Orb of Prophecy** | Magical gear, bosses (**Starlight Golem**, **Lunar Monstrosity**), **Chain of Souls** (Star) | Strong bosses; hostile biomes; use **Seeking Eyes** to find structures |
| **The Bumblezone** (The Bumblezone) | **Throw an Ender Pearl into a Beehive / Bee Nest** | 24/7 honey farming; **Crystalline Flower** (apply *any* enchantment to gear) | Drowning in honey; aggressive bees |

> **Not present as core progression:** standard ATM10 does **not** ship **Ad Astra** (space) or
> **The Aether** as progression dimensions. The canonical set is the eight above plus
> Overworld/Nether/End. Treat space/Aether as **absent unless a server adds them**. **✅** your
> install's mod list.

## Non-obvious access methods (newbie blockers)

- **Mining Dimension / The Other:** both use **Teleport Pads** crafted from **Allthemodium
  Nuggets** — you can't reach them until you have Allthemodium.
- **Twilight Forest:** the **diamond-in-a-flower-ringed-water-pool** portal is non-obvious;
  and the **"you are not worthy"** dark-forest barrier requires killing the **Lich** (which
  requires the **Naga** first). Bosses must be done roughly in order.
- **Bumblezone:** **ender pearl into a beehive** is not something you'd guess.
- **Eternal Starlight:** needs the **Orb of Prophecy**, not an obvious portal block.

## What each dimension gates (quick reference)

- **Allthemodium** → Deep Dark / Mining Dim
- **Vibranium** → Nether / The Other
- **Unobtainium** → The End; **Unobtainium templates** → The Other (only)
- **Heart of the Deep** (Wither's Compass component) → The Other
- **Chain of Souls** (Dragon Soul component) → Eternal Starlight
- **Crystalline Flower** (any-enchant for boss gear) → Bumblezone

> 🪝 **Build hooks.** The friction here is **discoverability** — every dimension has a quirky,
> un-guessable entry method, and the Twilight Forest boss-order wall stops people cold. A
> helper mod could add a "dimensional atlas" guide item that explains each portal recipe and
> prerequisite, or hand out the obscure portal igniters (Orb of Prophecy, a pre-made Twilight
> portal kit) as goodies. See [11](11-mod-build-opportunities.md).
