# 08 · Key Mods Reference

**Pack:** ATM10 · MC 1.21.1 · NeoForge. This is a **curated** list of the ~30 mods that
progression actually touches — not all ~500. Each notes its role and why it matters to the
ATM Star.

## Tools, gear & enchanting

| Mod | Role | Why it matters |
|---|---|---|
| **Silent Gear** | Modular tools/armor | Permanent, repairable, upgradeable gear; your pick for the whole game ([01](01-getting-started.md)) |
| **Allthemodium** | Gated ores + alloys | The material spine: Allthemodium/Vibranium/Unobtainium + alloys + indestructible alloy paxel ([02](02-progression-ladder.md)) |
| **Apotheosis** | RPG gear/affixes | Affix + gem/socket gear + World Tiers — how you get strong enough for bosses |
| **Apothic Enchanting** | Enchanting overhaul | Shelf-stacked high-tier enchants; produces **Infused Breath** (Dragon Soul) |

## Power

| Mod | Role | Why it matters |
|---|---|---|
| **Powah** | FE generation/storage | Simple scalable reactors + Energy Cells/cables; reliable early-mid power |
| **Mekanism** | Power + processing | Heat/gas gens, **Fission/Fusion**, plus the whole ore chain & **Digital Miner** & QIO |
| **Extreme Reactors** | Mega FE | Big multiblock reactors/turbines; **Insanite Block** is a Star ingredient |
| **Flux Networks** | Wireless FE | Moves power anywhere without cables; ties all generators together |
| **Generator Galore** | Niche generators | **Netherstar Generator 64×** & **Ender Generator** feed Star components |

## Ore, farming & resources

| Mod | Role | Why it matters |
|---|---|---|
| **Mekanism** | 2×–5× ore processing | Core ore multiplier ([03](03-ore-and-resources.md)) |
| **Mystical Agriculture** (+ **Mystical Agradditions**) | Grow resources | Inferium→Insanium crops; **Nether Star Crux**, **Witherproof Bricks**, **Creative Essence**, **Insanium Coal** for the Star |
| **Productive Bees** (+ **Productive Trees**) | Passive resources | **Soul Lava Bee**, **Withered Comb**, **Socotra Dragon Sapling**, **Starry Bees → ATM Star Shards** |
| **AllTheCompressed** | Compressed blocks | Bulk compressed mats for alloys, Dimensional Seed, Nether Star blocks |

## Storage & automation

| Mod | Role | Why it matters |
|---|---|---|
| **Applied Energistics 2** | Digital storage + autocraft | The endgame network that **autocrafts the ATM Star** (mind the channels) |
| **Refined Storage** | Digital storage (simple) | Channel-free alternative; easier start ([05](05-storage-automation.md)) |
| **Mega Cells / Advanced AE / AE2WTLib** | AE2 add-ons | Huge cells, threaders, wireless terminals — feed **Improbable Probability Device** & **Nexium Emitter** |
| **Create** | Mechanical automation | SU-based crushing/pressing/crafting; **required** for some Star sub-recipes |
| **Sophisticated Storage / Backpacks** | Early storage | Comfortable upgradeable chests/backpacks |
| **Occultism** | Magic + storage | No-power **Dimensional Storage**; **Soul Gem** + **Familiar Dragon** (Dragon Soul) |
| **Laser IO / Modular Routers / Pipez** | Logistics | Item/fluid/energy routing of every flavor |
| **Modern Industrialization** | Tech + multiblocks | Builds the **Runic Star Altar** itself; **Blastproof Casing**, motors feed Star parts |

## Combat, bosses & dimensions

| Mod | Role | Why it matters |
|---|---|---|
| **L_Ender's Cataclysm** | Bosses | Drops **Witherite, Ignitium, Cursium, Abyssal Sacrifice, Void Forge, Meat Shredder** — Star ingredients + top weapons |
| **Twilight Forest** | Boss dimension | Mid-game gear chain; **Snow Queen Trophy** (Oblivion Shard) |
| **Eternal Starlight** | Boss dimension | **Chain of Souls** (Dragon Soul) |
| **The Bumblezone** | Utility dimension | **Crystalline Flower** = apply any enchantment |
| **Deeper and Darker** | Dimension/ore | Adds **The Other** (Unobtainium templates) + **Heart of the Deep** (Wither's Compass) |
| **Industrial Foregoing** | Mob farm/automation | **Mob Duplicator** (Pink Slime), **Wither Builder**, **Dissolution Chamber** (alloy) |
| **Hostile Neural Networks** | Mob loot | **Ender Dragon Data Model** (Dragon Soul) |
| **Mob Grinding Utils** | Physical mob farms | XP + drops |

## QoL (everyday navigability)

**JEI** (recipes), **JADE** (block/mob tooltips), **FTB Quests** (guidance), **Waystones**
(travel), **Iron Jetpacks** (flight), **Functional Storage** (drawers), **Building Gadgets /
Construction Wand / FramedBlocks** (mass placement + cosmetics).

> 🪝 **Build hooks.** A helper add-on most naturally **depends on / integrates with** this
> set — adding recipes that consume early items from these mods and emit QoL goodies, and
> JEI/JADE info pages. See [11](11-mod-build-opportunities.md).
