# 07 · Combat, Bosses & Magic

**Pack:** ATM10 · MC 1.21.1 · NeoForge. Recipe/drop facts marked **✅ JEI** / verify in-game.

Several ATM Star components come from **boss drops** and **magic mods**, so combat isn't
optional. You also need strong gear ([Apotheosis](#apotheosis--apothic-enchanting)) to
survive the [dimensions](06-dimensions.md).

## L'Ender's Cataclysm bosses (L_Ender's Cataclysm)

Eight custom bosses; several drops are **direct ATM Star ingredients** or unlock top weapons.
Common kill order: **Netherite Monstrosity → Ender Guardian → Harbinger → Leviathan → Scylla
→ Maledictus → Ignis → Ancient Remnant.**

| Boss | Where / summon | Key drops | Why fight it |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Netherite Monstrosity** | Nether (melee, throws lava) | **Burning Ashes** | Summons Ignis |
| **Ender Guardian** | Ruined Citadel | **Gauntlet of Guard** | Strong gauntlet; Void Cores |
| **The Harbinger** | **Ancient Factory** (Overworld); **woken with a Nether Star** | **Witherite** | **Only** Witherite source → Wither weapons; feeds **Wither's Compass** |
| **Ignis** | Summoned with Burning Ashes | **3× Ignitium Ingot** | Fire-immune Ignitium gear, **Incinerator** greatsword |
| **Maledictus** | Cursed structure | **Cursium Ingots** | Cursium gear |
| **The Leviathan** | Ocean-monument style | **Tidal Claws**, **Abyssal Sacrifice** | **Abyssal Sacrifice** feeds the **Dragon Soul** |
| **Ancient Remnant / Scylla / Wadjet** | **Cursed Pyramid** | High-tier loot, Ancient Spear | Hardest fights; pyramid clear → **Void Forge**, **Meat Shredder** (feed **Oblivion Shard**) |

> ⚠️ **Known pack bug:** Cataclysm bosses have had reports of **regenerating infinitely /
> being unkillable** (issue #2559) from cross-mod regen/damage-cap interactions. Fight in
> **melee with high DPS**; avoid relying on certain ranged/DoT damage. **✅** in v7.0.

## Apotheosis & Apothic Enchanting

- **Apotheosis (Apotheosis)** — loot-RPG overhaul. Gear rolls **randomized affixes** across
  **5 rarities (Common→Mythic)**; **Sockets + Gems** add more; workstations **salvage,
  reforge, augment, upgrade gem purity**. **5 World Tiers** scale difficulty + rewards and
  spawn **Apothic Invaders** with guaranteed drops. *This is how you build gear strong enough
  to beat the bosses.*
- **Apothic Enchanting** — overhauls enchanting. Stack **enchanting shelves** (Seashelf /
  Deepshelf / Endshelf families) around the table to raise **Eterna (level), Quanta
  (variance), Arcana (rarity)** for far higher enchants than vanilla. Produces **Infused
  Breath**, used in the **Dragon Soul**.
- **Crystalline Flower (The Bumblezone)** — the cheat-code: apply **any** enchantment
  (including **Decrepitude** for boss fights), bypassing normal limits.

## Mob farming (replace manual grinding)

- **Hostile Neural Networks (Hostile Networks)** — train **Data Models** in a **Deep Learner**,
  then mass-produce mob loot in **Simulation Chambers** (incl. the **Ender Dragon Data Model**
  for the Dragon Soul). ⚠️ Known ATM10 bugs where models reset/don't level (issues #1034,
  #1395) — **back up your models**.
- **Mob Grinding Utils** — classic physical farms: fans, spawners, **Absorption Hopper**, XP.
- **Industrial Foregoing** — **Mob Crusher** + **Mob Duplicator** (byproduct **Pink Slime**, an
  alloy ingredient) and the **Wither Builder** (auto-spawns Withers for Nether Stars — also a
  Star ingredient).

## Magic mods & their progression role

- **Ars Nouveau** — build **custom spells from glyphs**; **Source** power, flight, storage,
  mob farming. Provides the **Wither Glyph** (Wither's Compass) and the **Enchanting
  Apparatus** that makes the **Unobtainium-Allthemodium alloy**. Strong early-mid utility.
- **Occultism** — ritual/summoning. **Dimensional Storage** (AE-lite, **no power** — great
  mid-game), demon miners, **Familiars**; supplies **Soul Gem** + **Familiar Dragon** for the
  Dragon Soul.
- **Iron's Spells 'n Spellbooks** — ready-made combat magic; supplies the **Wither Skull
  Scroll** (Wither's Compass) and **Lightning Upgrade Orb** (Improbable Probability Device).
- **Productive Trees / Productive Bees** — grow the **Socotra Dragon Sapling** and breed the
  **Soul Lava Bee**, **Withered Comb**, and **Starry Bees** (ATM Star Shards).
- **EvilCraft**, **Forbidden & Arcanus**, **Just Dire Things**, **Roots Classic** — each
  supply specific ATM Star sub-ingredients (see [09](09-atm-star.md)).

> 🪝 **Build hooks.** Combat is where low-skill players hard-stop: the Warden, Cataclysm
> bosses (sometimes literally unkillable due to bugs), and needing Apotheosis gear they don't
> understand. A helper mod could ship a "training armor" goodie set with solid defensive
> affixes, a simple boss-prep checklist item, and a guaranteed-melee-friendly weapon to get
> people past the first bosses. See [11](11-mod-build-opportunities.md).
