## How to read this as a builder

- Pages **00–08** establish the systems and vocabulary (exact item names + owning mod in
  `(Mod Name)` form).
- Page **09** is the destination — the ATM Star tree — and is the densest reference.
- Pages **10–11** are the design payload: every friction point in [10](10-newcomer-friction.md)
  has at least one proposed mitigation in [11](11-mod-build-opportunities.md).

## Conventions

- Items are written **Item Name (Mod Name)**; IDs are given as `namespace:id` where known.
- **✅ JEI** = verify this exact recipe/quantity in-game; it is version-sensitive.
- **🪝 Build hooks** call out where a helper add-on could plausibly intervene.
- Each page ends with a **Sources** list.
