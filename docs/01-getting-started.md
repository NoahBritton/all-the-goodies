# 01 · Getting Started (the first few hours)

**Pack:** ATM10 · MC 1.21.1 · NeoForge. Recipe facts marked **✅ JEI**.

The goal of day one is: a tool that lasts forever, doubled ore yields, fast travel, reliable
food, and early flight. Open the **FTB Quest Book** (top-left of inventory) first — it pays
you to follow it.

## Tools — Silent Gear

**Silent Gear** is the backbone tool/armor mod for the whole game.

- You build **modular tools** from **parts** using a **blueprint/template** in a crafting
  station. **You must craft the blueprint first** — parts won't combine without it (a common
  early snag). **✅ JEI** the blueprint recipes.
- Silent Gear tools are effectively **permanent**: infinitely repairable with **Repair Kits
  (Silent Gear)**, and **upgraded by swapping parts** rather than rebuilt. A good Silent Gear
  pickaxe is meant to **last the entire game** — invest in one early.
- Tool tier matters for the [metal ladder](02-progression-ladder.md): you'll want a
  **Netherite-tier** Silent Gear pickaxe to mine Allthemodium.

## Vein Miner (FTB Ultimine)

The pack ships **FTB Ultimine** — everyone calls it "Vein Miner."

- **Default keybind: grave / backtick `` ` ``.** **Hold** it while mining to break a whole
  connected vein or tree.
- **Shift + scroll** while holding changes **mode** (Shapeless, Small Tunnel, Staircase, …).
- Snag: it's a *hold* key, and rebinding the grave key loses it. **✅** the Controls menu.

## Ore Hammer — easy 2× ore

Craft an **Ore Hammer** (e.g. **Copper Ore Hammer**) early to **double raw ore** before you
have machines. Right-click/process ore to get 2× output. This is the cheapest ore-doubling
in the game and bridges you to [Mekanism processing](03-ore-and-resources.md). **✅ JEI**.

## Waystones — fast travel

**Waystones (Waystones)** generate in **villages** (and camps/ships).

- **Right-click to activate** a Waystone, then teleport free between any you've activated.
- Strategy: activate **every** Waystone you pass to build a travel network.
- Snag: leaving without activating one means you can't warp back to it.

## Food & farming

Food comes mainly from **Farmer's Delight** and **Pam's HarvestCraft 2**.

- Skip vanilla wheat at first — forage **wild crops, wild onions, and fruit trees** near
  spawn for instant food.
- Craft a **Farmer's Delight knife + cooking pot**; cooked meals give strong saturation.
- If you find an **Infinity Ham** (relic/loot drop), keep it — it's effectively infinite food.

## Early flight (jetpacks)

- Cheapest: **Wooden Jetpack (Iron Jetpacks)** — planks + basics, runs on **FE**. Gives
  vertical lift/hover early. Pair with **Free Runners (Mekanism)** for movement.
- Later: **Mekanism Jetpack / Armored Jetpack** (hydrogen-fueled). The **Armored Jetpack**
  also gives ~12 armor points (better than Prot IV diamond chest) and buffers ~24,000
  hydrogen (~20/sec while flying).

Other QoL mods in the family: backpacks/drawers, Curios slots, trash/magnet utilities —
**✅** their exact names/keybinds in **Controls → "All the Mods 10"** (these vary by version).

> 🪝 **Build hooks.** Day one is where low-skill players bounce: they don't craft the Silent
> Gear blueprint, miss Vein Miner, or starve. A "starter goodie bag" (see
> [11](11-mod-build-opportunities.md)) handed out by the first quest — pre-made Silent Gear
> pick, a stack of food, an ore hammer, a Wooden Jetpack — would smooth the entire opening.
